Rayyan has experimented with coffee ever since he was a sophomore in high school. From your standard instant grounds to tinkering with an old-school espresso machine in his free time. **Rayyan treats the art of coffee as his greatest passion!**

Rayyan also enjoys dabbling in the art of making _cold brew._ A speical kind of coffee that is brewed with cold water instead of your typical hot-brewing method. Read more about the art of making cold brew [here](https://www.aboutcoffee.org/brewing/cold-brew-coffee/)
